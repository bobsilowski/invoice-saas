Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 06, 2025

Welcome to InvoiceAI. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our invoicing service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our service:

  • Personal Information: Your name, phone number, and email address, which you provide when filling out the invoicing form.
  • Invoice Data: Details such as client name, due date, billable items, and bank details (if provided) to generate your invoice.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate and provide you with invoices in PDF format.
  • Contact you if you’ve provided an email address, for example, to notify you of updates or issues with your invoice.
  • Improve our service by analyzing usage patterns (anonymized data only).

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Netlify’s servers when you submit the form. We do not store your invoice data on our servers after the PDF is generated; it is stored locally in your browser’s memory and cleared when you refresh the page. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to comply with legal obligations.

5. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

If you are in the European Union, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: You can request that we correct any inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your data (note: we do not store your data after the session ends).
  • Objection: You can object to the processing of your data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@invoiceai.com.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies on this site. Your data is processed locally in your browser.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@invoiceai.com.
